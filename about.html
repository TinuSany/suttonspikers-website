About the club

A local volleyball club with energy, warmth, and room to grow.

Sutton Spikers Volleyball Club is a vibrant and welcoming community founded by Malayalee players from Croydon, Sutton, Epsom, and Redhill.

Founded 1 January 2025 Serving Sutton and Surrey Volleyball England affiliated

Our club identity

Sutton Spikers was founded by Malayalee players who wanted to create a welcoming volleyball community for people across Sutton, Surrey, and the surrounding area. The club is built around the idea that local sport should feel inviting, energetic, and genuinely community-led.

We are passionate about growing the game and bringing people together through volleyball. Whether someone is returning to the sport, improving their game, or looking for a regular team environment, the club should feel approachable from the very first visit.

Club colours and kit

Two distinct looks built around the Sutton Spikers blue-and-yellow identity.

Our playing kit carries the club colours proudly, with a strong blue home look and a bold yellow libero design that stands out on court.

Main kit

Primary blue match kit

The main jersey leads with the club's strong royal blue, finished with yellow detailing that ties directly back to the Sutton Spikers badge and sponsor presentation.

Libero kit

High-contrast yellow alternate kit

The libero design flips the palette beautifully, using vibrant yellow as the hero colour while keeping the signature club blue visible through the shoulders, crest, and trim.
