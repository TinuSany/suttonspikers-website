Why Sutton Spikers

Built on community, identity, and a love of the game.

Founded on 1 January 2025, Sutton Spikers brings together a growing volleyball community across Sutton and Surrey. The club is affiliated with Volleyball England and the Surrey Volleyball Association, and continues to build a welcoming space for committed adult players who want to train, compete, and grow together.
