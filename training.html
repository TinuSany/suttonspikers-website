Who can join

Adults with solid playing experience

Sutton Spikers currently supports players aged 18 and over only. At the moment, we are best suited to intermediate and advanced players who would enjoy joining practice games with the team.

Coaching update

No qualified coach just yet

We do not have a qualified coach at the moment, so we are not able to accept beginners right now. We are hopeful of bringing in a coach from next season, starting in August 2026.

Match calendar

Training can occasionally change

Because the team competes in Surrey Men's Division 3, some Sunday practice sessions may occasionally be cancelled around matches.
